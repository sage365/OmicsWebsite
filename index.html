PAPER AND POSTER PRESENTATION

Rules:

1. The abstract should be brief containing 300 words.
2. The abstracts for paper and poster presentation should be mailed to omics2k17@gmail.com with their team name and title.
3. A team of maximum 2 members can participate.
4. Research papers will be selected for the paper presentation and the rest will be selected for the poster presentation.

Topics:

1. Molecular biology
2. Bioprocess/downstream processing
3. Cell biology
4. Bioinformatics and Computational biology
5. Systems biology


Deadline For Submission Of Abstract: 17th September, 2017
